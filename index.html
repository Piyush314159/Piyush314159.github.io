04

COVID-19 Epidemic Modeling — SIR / SEIR / SUTRA

Completed

Implemented and compared SIR, SEIR, and the SUTRA compartmental model (which explicitly tracks undetected/asymptomatic carriers) on US national COVID-19 data (Feb 2020 – Mar 2021). Used NNLS regression for automatic per-phase parameter estimation of contact rate β̃ and reach ρ̃, with a product-R² fallback for collinear phases, achieving MAPE as low as ~4% on individual phases. Ensembled all three models via linear regression for improved overall fit.
